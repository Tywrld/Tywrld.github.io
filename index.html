About Us

"Help-Hub" is a website designed to help people connect with charities that align with their interests and values. It offers a searchable directory of various nonprofits, categorized by focus areas such as education, environment, health, and social justice. Users can browse, learn about different charities, and easily sign up to volunteer or donate. The platform also provides tools for tracking involvement and recommending causes based on user preferences, making it easier for people to find meaningful ways to give back to their communities.
